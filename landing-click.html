Our Mission

Phyllis Software provides high-quality, custom software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of small businesses. With a focus on innovation, efficiency, and personalized services, we help small businesses push the standard forward, enabling them to succeed in the competitive digital landscape.

About the Founder

Elijah Thomas, a veteran, software engineer, and entrepreneur, has always had a personal passion for Information Systems, web applications, and Server Administration. This passion has given him a unique perspective and a strong desire to serve his community by using his skills to help small businesses grow and compete with global competitors.

Our Values

Phyllis Software is dedicated to empowering small businesses and underrepresented markets by providing affordable, practical, and scalable software solutions that help bring their ideas to life. We are committed to honesty, integrity, and delivering exceptional value to our clients and future products, enabling them to thrive in the global digital economy.
